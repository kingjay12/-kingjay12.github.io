How to Make Hotdogs

  1. First the hotdog bun
  2. Then you cook the hotdog in the microwave or on stove top
  3. Then after you use your prefered cooking method you put the hotdog in the bun
  4. Finally you top the hotdog with what ever toppings you would like and eat while warm
